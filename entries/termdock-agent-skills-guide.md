## Why it's here

Covers what skill composition looks like at scale, beyond a single
SKILL.md. Uses the Superpowers framework (Jesse Vincent) as its main
example of many skills combined into a full methodology.

## Key claim or contribution

Distinguishes personal skills (private workflow habits) from project
skills (shared team conventions) as separate scopes with different
audiences and different update discipline. Also argues for a "one
skill, one verb" rule: split a skill when it tries to serve two
different trigger patterns or produce two different kinds of output.

## Who should read/watch this

Anyone with more than 3-4 skills who's starting to feel like they
overlap or step on each other.
